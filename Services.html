Services

Services at Sweet Speech are offered exclusively through teletherapy. Whitney will connect with you via a secure Zoom account to provide speech services for your child. Although, Whitney considers her specializatio to be working with children who have difficultly producing speech sounds and are difficult to understand, these are not the only services that she is qualified to offer. See the list of services Sweet Speech can provide for your child below.

Services Offered

  • Treating Speech Sound Disorders & Delays
  • Treating Receptive Language
  • Treating Expressive Language
  • Implementing AAC (Alternative and Augmentative Communication)
